About BoxCharter

Making Charts

Use BoxCharter to make music charts with chords and lyrics (see example). Creating a chart is simple:

  1. Create sections and measures.
  2. Configure the chart with specs such as:
    • how many measures to display per row
    • how many beats (or points at which the chord may change) per measure
  3. Add chords and lyrics.
  4. Download a PDF of your creation to share with friends.

Why do I need an account?

In order to save your charts, they must be associated with a user account.
